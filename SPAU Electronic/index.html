Our Story

Lighting Your World with Quality LED Solutions

Established in 2016, SPAU Electronics Pvt. Ltd. has been dedicated to designing and manufacturing high-quality LED products, from bulbs and battens to smart lights and floodlights. Our expert team ensures each product is crafted with precision, meeting rigorous quality standards in our in-house laboratory. We offer a wide range of LED solutions from 3W to 80W, in multiple colors, ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications. By sourcing premium materials from trusted vendors, we promise products that stand the test of time and enhance any space with reliable, energy-efficient lighting.
